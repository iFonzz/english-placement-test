Choose the correct answer (A, B, C, or D).

1. What time do you usually get up on weekdays?  
A. At home  
B. At 7 o’clock  
C. I am tired  
D. I don’t late  
ANSWER: B

2. My brother doesn’t like pizza, but I ____.  
A. do  
B. did  
C. does  
D. am  
ANSWER: A

3. A: "Where’s your phone?"

A. am losing  
B. lose  
C. lost  
D. have lost  
ANSWER: D
